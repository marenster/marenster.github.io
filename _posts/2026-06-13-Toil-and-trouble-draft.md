YOU GUYS. 

I know I've been away for a while, but YOU GUYS. 

I have a book contract! 

Here is the ![Publisher's Marketplace announcement. ](/img/PMDealReport_524464.png "PMDeal Toil and Trouble") I was so excitecd, I made my agent take a screenshot of it. The text reads:

TOIL AND TROUBLE by Maren Anderson
Publisher: Bloomsbury Academic
Western [Oregon] University instructor Maren Anderson's TOIL AND TROUBLE, analyizing witches in historical literature and legends from around the world, and how their depiction is a reflection of both the patriarchy and how women were viewed in each time and place, to Christen Karniski at Bloomsbury Academic for publication fall 2027, by Elisa Saphier at McGregor and Luedeke (world English). 

In other words, I look at witches and other magical women in literature, fairy tales, and stories and then compare the way the fictional women are treated to how real-life women of the time were treated. 

It's been fascinating. 

So since this time last year, I have been writing this book. Now I am finishing the first draft of my final chapter! This summer, I will polish the whole book and track down stray references and image rights. I turn the whole thing in on September 15, 2026.

You can find it [here](https://witchoftheweek.substack.com/p/coming-soon), but I may not have posted anything yet.
